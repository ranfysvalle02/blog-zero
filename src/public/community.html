Community Guidelines & Terms

Our Community

blog-zero is a space for sharing ideas, learning, and thoughtful conversation. Everyone who participates — whether reading, writing, or commenting — is part of this community. We ask that you help us keep it welcoming and constructive.

Comment Guidelines

When leaving comments, please follow these principles:

  • Be respectful. Disagree with ideas, not people. No personal attacks, harassment, or hate speech of any kind.
  • Be constructive. Add to the conversation. If you disagree, explain why thoughtfully.
  • Stay on topic. Keep comments relevant to the post you're responding to.
  • No spam or self-promotion. Don't use comments to advertise products, services, or unrelated links.
  • No harmful content. Do not post content that is illegal, threatening, obscene, or violates anyone's rights.
  • Protect privacy. Never share personal information about yourself or others (phone numbers, addresses, etc.).

All comments require admin approval before they appear publicly. Comments that violate these guidelines will not be approved and may result in account restrictions.

Terms of Use

By using this blog, you agree to the following:

  • You are responsible for any content you post, including comments.
  • You grant blog-zero a non-exclusive right to display your comments alongside the posts they respond to.
  • We reserve the right to remove any content or restrict any account that violates these guidelines, at our sole discretion.
  • We may update these guidelines at any time. Continued use of the blog constitutes acceptance of any changes.

Privacy

We take your privacy seriously. Here's what we collect and why:

  • Account info: Your email and name are stored to identify your account and display your comments. We do not share this with third parties.
  • Comments: Your comments are stored and displayed publicly (after approval). You can request deletion of your comments by contacting the admin.
  • Cookies: We use a session cookie for authentication and a local storage key for your theme preference. No tracking cookies, no analytics.
  • No third-party tracking: We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other third-party tracking service.

Contact

If you have concerns about content on this blog, want to report a violation, or request deletion of your data, reach out to the site administrator.
